What we have here is essentially a visual representation of our grid layout. The letters there are just to name each column on each row of our grid. The `h` is for header, `m` is for menu, `c` is for content, and `f` is for footer. These names are arbitrary, the `h` could just as well be named `header` or `dolphinpants`. But best practice is to name it in a way that allows you to clearly understand its relation to its column, so in this case `h` is for header.
